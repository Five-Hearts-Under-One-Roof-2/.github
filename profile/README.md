## Description  
**Five Hearts Under One Roof 2** is the sequel in the interactive romance simulation series by Storytaco. In the first game, the player, as Yuman, becomes the manager of a boarding house shared with five unique heroines. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  
Production of the second entry has been confirmed by the developer. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Expect expanded storylines, new characters, and more branching endings.

---

## Key Features  
- First-person immersive romance simulation with FMV/live-action scenes. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}  
- Multiple heroines with distinct personalities and story paths (childhood friend, former idol, boss, younger girl, shy recluse). :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}  
- Rich branching narrative and multiple endings based on your choices. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}  
- High production value, realistic video content and acting. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}  
- Sequel positioning: original main characters return, new scenarios expected. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
